# Collaboration & Cross-Promotion

Grow by borrowing trust from aligned creators. The indie maker ecosystem runs on collaboration: newsletter cross-promotions, podcast guest circuits (Startups for the Rest of Us, IndieHackers Podcast, Bootstrapped Web), MicroConf speaker networks, and co-created content between founders. Collaboration is the fastest organic growth lever because you reach a pre-qualified audience that already trusts the person introducing you.

## Finding the Right Partners

Look for creators who share your audience but not your exact topic:

| Your Niche | Good Partner Niche | Why |
|------------|-------------------|-----|
| AI tools for developers | Developer productivity | Same audience, different angle |
| Personal finance | Career growth | Financial goals connect to earning more |
| No-code building | Solo founder marketing | Both serve the same builder persona |

## Partner Evaluation Criteria

Before reaching out, check:

- [ ] Similar audience size (0.5x to 3x your size works best)
- [ ] Aligned values and tone (would you recommend their content to your audience?)
- [ ] Active engagement (follower count means nothing if nobody comments)
- [ ] History of collaboration (have they done this before?)
- [ ] No direct competition on your core topic

## Collaboration Formats

Drawn from patterns in the indie maker community:

| Format | Effort | Reach | Best For |
|--------|--------|-------|----------|
| Newsletter swap | Low | Medium | Email list cross-pollination (Bootstrappers.io model) |
| Guest post | Medium | High | SEO + authority building |
| Podcast guesting | Medium | High | Long-form trust building (IndieHackers, SaaS Bootstrapper) |
| Co-created content | High | Very High | Unique assets neither could make alone |
| Joint launch | Very High | Very High | Product or course launches |
| Affiliate partnership | Low | Ongoing | Passive cross-referral |

## Outreach Templates

**Cold outreach (keep it short):**

> Hey [Name], I have been following your work on [specific thing]. I create content about [your topic] for [your audience]. I think our audiences overlap well. Would you be open to [specific collab idea]? Happy to share more details if you are interested.

**Warm outreach (after engaging with their content):**

> Hey [Name], loved your recent [post/episode] on [topic], especially [specific insight]. I have been thinking about [related angle] and think we could create something valuable together. Would a [format] make sense? I have [your audience size/proof point] and our audiences seem aligned.

Rules: Always lead with what you admire about their work. Always propose a specific format. Never send a generic "let's collab" message.

## Podcast Guesting

The indie podcast circuit (Startups for the Rest of Us, Mixergy, How I Built This, IndieHackers) is one of the most effective authority-building channels:

1. Build a "media kit" page: bio, headshot, 3-5 topic bullets, past appearances
2. Target shows with 10-500 episodes (active but still booking guests)
3. Pitch a specific story or framework, not just "I can talk about X"
4. Prepare 2-3 memorable soundbites in advance
5. Share the episode with your audience and tag the host

## Joint Launches

Joint launches multiply reach but require trust and coordination:

- Agree on shared goals, timeline, and promotion commitments upfront
- Each partner promotes to their audience with authentic endorsement
- Create a shared landing page or co-branded asset
- Split revenue or leads fairly based on contribution
- Debrief afterward: what worked, what to do differently

## Affiliate Partnerships

Low-effort, ongoing cross-promotion:

- Recommend products and tools you genuinely use
- Offer reciprocal affiliate arrangements with aligned creators
- Disclose affiliate relationships transparently
- Track which partnerships drive real value (not just clicks)
