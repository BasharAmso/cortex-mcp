# Content Repurposing System

Create once, distribute many times. One pillar piece becomes 10+ assets. This mirrors the production pipeline approach: structured phases with clear inputs and outputs.

## The Repurposing Tree

```
Long-Form Pillar (blog post, video, podcast)
  +-- 3-5 Social Posts (key takeaways, one per point)
  +-- 1 Thread / Carousel (step-by-step breakdown)
  +-- 1 Email Newsletter (summary + personal angle)
  +-- 2-3 Short Videos / Reels (punchy clips)
  +-- 1 Infographic or Visual (data or process diagram)
  +-- 1 Quote Graphic (best one-liner)
  +-- 1 Community Post (discussion prompt)
```

## Format Conversion Rules

| Source | Target | What to Extract |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Blog post** | Thread | One section per post, hook with boldest claim |
| **Blog post** | Carousel | Steps become slides, add visuals |
| **Podcast** | Short video | Best 60-second clip, add captions |
| **Podcast** | Blog post | Transcribe, clean up, add structure |
| **Video** | Quote graphic | Screenshot strongest moment, overlay text |
| **Newsletter** | Social post | Lead with the takeaway readers replied to most |

## Quality Rules

1. **Each piece must stand alone.** Never post something that only makes sense if you read the original.
2. **Adapt the hook.** What works as a blog intro does not work as a tweet opener. Rewrite the first line for each platform.
3. **Respect the format.** A carousel is visual. A thread is conversational. A reel is punchy. Don't copy-paste into a different box.
4. **Add native value.** Include a platform-specific insight, question, or CTA that the original didn't have.

## Workflow Template

| Step | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
| 1 | Publish pillar piece | Day 1 |
| 2 | Pull 3-5 standalone takeaways | Day 1 (15 min) |
| 3 | Write thread or carousel | Day 2 (20 min) |
| 4 | Record 1-2 short video clips | Day 3 (15 min) |
| 5 | Send newsletter with personal angle | Day 4 |
| 6 | Schedule remaining social posts | Day 4 (10 min) |

## Anti-Patterns

- Copy-pasting the same text everywhere (audiences notice)
- Repurposing before the pillar piece is strong (garbage in, garbage out)
- Skipping the hook rewrite (the #1 reason repurposed content underperforms)
- Trying to repurpose everything (pick your best 1-2 pieces per week)
